PROJECTS

A Dystopian Murder Show

In A Dystopian Murder Show, a 2D action platformer game, players control two mutants sharing one body and try to survive in deadly arenas..

Blinded

Set in a city that experiences a blackout, you play as a hacker that tries to fix the city, taking back the infrastructure piece by piece by solving different puzzles located in the game world.

The Messy Genius

The Messy Genius is a puzzle game designed for touch devices (e.g. Tablets, Mobile) and was created at the Beethoven Game Jam 2019.

In game, there are various colour-coded note sheets falling down and the player needs to catch and subsequently drag them to the right coloured spot of a stave located in the middle of the screen.
