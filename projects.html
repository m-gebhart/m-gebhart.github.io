Blinded (2019)

The Messy Genius (2019)

The Messy Genius is a puzzle game designed for touch devices (e.g. Tablets, Mobile) and was created at the Beethoven Game Jam 2019.

In game, there are various colour-coded note sheets falling down and the player needs to catch and subsequently drag them to the right coloured spot of a stave located in the middle of the screen.
