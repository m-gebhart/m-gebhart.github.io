A Dystopian Murder Show

2019 · 2D Platformer Action · Collaborative Project (8 Weeks)

In A Dystopian Murder Show, a 2D action platformer game, players control two mutants sharing one body. Trapped in deadly arenas, they try to survive against brutal hordes of monsters and get the highest score as possible.

My responsibilities were implementing a majority of game systems, including:

  • Player Systems and Controls, including Xbox Game Pad Support
  • Enemy Behaviour and Combat Systems
  • UI, Menu Systems, Scene Management and Camera
  • Animation Systems
  • Tools (/Unity Prefabs) for a more efficient creation of levels
  • Trailer editing in Vegas Movie Studio 14

The downloadable link can be found on itch.io

Team Members: Isaac Baaltsch (Game Design) | Yannick Renz (Game Design, Sound Design) | Angelo Wedel (Arts & Animation) | Ioana Sandu (Arts & Animation) | Michael Gebhart (Programming)
