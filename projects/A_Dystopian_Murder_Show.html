A Dystopian Murder Show

2019 · 2D Platformer Action · Collaborative Project (8 Weeks)

In A Dystopian Murder Show, a 2D action platformer game, players control two mutants sharing one body. Trapped in deadly arenas, they try to survive against brutal hordes of monsters and get the highest score as possible.

The overall design is inspired by 1940s comedy slapstick movies. The shapeshifting mechanic can be executed at any time and offers various play-styles. Furthermore, we focussed on creating an intuitive combat and movement system.

In a team of five, I was the leading programmer and took care of all major programming aspects to realize this project in Unity.


The downloadable link to itch.io can be found here


My Role: Game Systems and Tools Programming (Input Systems, UI, Camera, Enemies, Combat, Animation States and Level Design Tools)
