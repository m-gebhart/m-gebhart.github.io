A Dystopian Murder Show

2019 · 2D Platformer Action · Collaborative Project - 8 Weeks

In A Dystopian Murder Show, a 2D action platformer game, players control two mutants sharing one body and try to survive in deadly arenas.

My Role: Game and Tools Programming; implementing Player Controls / Input Systems, UI, Camera, Enemy Systems, Combat Mechanics, Animation States and Tools for Level Designers in Unity.

Team Members: Isaac Baaltsch (Game Design), Yannick Renz (Game Design, Sound Design), Angelo Wedel (Arts & Animation), Ioana Sandu (Arts & Animation), Michael Gebhart (Programming)
