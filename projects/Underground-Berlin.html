Underground Berlin

2020 · ARG · Collaborative Project (8 Weeks)

Undergound Berlin is an Alternate Reality Game (ARG) based on a proof a concept for a web series. Its puzzles are transmedial, meaning that they are spread across various devices such as physical documents and online webpages.

My responsibilities were:

  • Pitching initial design concept
  • Creating static websites with JavaScript, HTML, CSS and JEKYLL

Team Members: Lars Kalthoff (Narrative Design) | Theodore Menezes (Narrative Design) | Ioana Sandu (Graphic Design) | Lucas Donnard (Creative Direction) | Michael Gebhart (Programming)
