Messy Genius

2019 · 2D Action Puzzle · Game Jam Project (48h)

In game, there are various colour-coded note sheets falling down and the player needs to catch and subsequently drag them to the right coloured spot of a stave located in the middle of the screen.

It was developed at the Beethoven Game Jam 2019.

My Role: Game Programming (Input systems and overall game logic)

Team Members: Daniel Baracotti (Game Design, Sound Design, Audio Programming) | Stefan Pohl (Arts & Animation) | Michael Gebhart (Programming)
