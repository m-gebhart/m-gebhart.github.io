Blinded

2019 - 2D Sidescrolling Adventure with Puzzle Elements

Set in a city that experiences a blackout, you play as a hacker that tries to fix the city, taking back the infrastructure piece by piece by solving different puzzles located in the game world.

Game and Audio Programming; UI, Sound, Additional Graphical Effects, Shaders and Animations. Emily Wysocki (Lead, Game Design, Sound Design), Alexandru Negoescu (Gameplay Programming), Anastasiia Tataurova (2D Arts & Animation), Kaya Golder (3d Arts & Animation), Lucia Zhai (Supporting Artist), Fabian Schreck (Soundtrack)
