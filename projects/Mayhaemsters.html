Mayhaemsters

2020 · Multiplayer Action Game · Collaborative Project (8 Weeks) · Unreal Engine 4 / C++

Mayhaemsters is a last-man-standing competitive multiplayer game for up to four players where your playstyle not only influences the layout of the map, but also the way your friends interact with their own controls.

All in all, Mayhaemsters should surprise their players with breaking conventions:
The map provides major environmental elements that can morph out of the ground when triggered by your attacks’ projectiles. Furthermore, you can pick up various power-ups including one that remaps the key bindings of your enemies’ four basic abilities across the same buttons causing uncertainty and chaos.

The game is designed to be a thrilling and fun experience that relieves the spirit of ludic couch multiplayer games.

My responsibilities as a producer and second programmer were:

  • Pitching initial design concept
  • Managing development process by guiding and overseeing tasks
  • Editing gameplay videos and course presentations with Vegas Movie Studio
  • Implementing systems for UI, audio and animations with Blueprints
  • Improving and tweaking existing mechanics (e.g. movement) with C++

We are planning to release this game on major platforms in the future. Thus, no code is provided.


Team Members: Najeeb Mshaweh (Gameplay Programming) | Daniel Baracotti (Game Design & Sound Design) | Hanafy Mahmoud & Sebestian Gerena (Game Arts) | Sameh Aransa (Arts Consulting) | Michael Gebhart (Production & Programming)
