Take Your Time

2019 · Narrative Adventure · Game Jam Project (3 Days, 10 to 18)

Take Your Time is a Narrative Adventure and Walking Simulator game where you get to see different sections of life while discovering all the stories hidden in the cars of a traffic jam. The further you progress in that traffic jam, the closer you get to a dead's end

My responsibility was:

  • Visual Scripting in Unreal Engine 4

It was developed at the Game Jam 2.0: The Art of Taking a Walk that took place at Cologne Game Lab back in December 2019.

Team Members: Anton Sagel (Game Design, Level Design) | Nare Matevosian (Level Arts & Programming) | Michael Gebhart (Programming)
