ABOUT ME

I'm a game programmer from Germany, currently enrolled in the Digital Games Bachelor program at Cologne Game Lab, an institute of the University of Applied Sciences Cologne (/TH Koeln). For my studies, I focus on Game Programming, other technological aspects of interactive media and the realization of several collaborative projects in each semester.

 For life, I intend to be a part of a talented team that makes games pushing forward genres and the medium as a whole. A team, that puts years and years of hard work and effort into a project that impacts people - just like all those games everyone of us used to grow up with.

 Games can reach and embrace audiences in a way that no other medium is able to. And I'm excited to find out what marks I can leave in this challenging, but great and vast industry.
